Company Overview

A Legacy of
Music Excellence

Established on 1 July 2025 and headquartered in Sydney, NSW, The Oz Soundz Group is a premier collective of music-focused enterprises. Our group integrates three specialised divisions to support the global and Australian music industry.

The Oz Soundz Group operates as a subsidiary of Vestinex Pty Ltd (ABN: 45 147 744 858), an Australian-owned private company with a proven track record of professional excellence since 2010.

While our roots are firmly planted in the Australian music industry, our vision is global — a driving force for creative professionals across music, television, and film.

Our Heritage

Four Decades of
Musical Passion

Founded by Jason "Plummo" Plumridge and his family, The Oz Soundz Group is built on over 40 years of grassroots industry experience. Our founder's journey began at the Newcastle Conservatorium of Music, sparking a multi-generational family legacy.

From keyboardists active in the Sydney music scene to accomplished singers and session drummers, music is the lifeblood of our leadership team. This deep-rooted heritage ensures that every service we provide is backed by authentic industry knowledge and a genuine passion for artistic success.
