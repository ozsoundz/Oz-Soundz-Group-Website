Empowering Grassroots Musicians

We Back
The Underdogs

At The Oz Soundz Group, we don't just provide services — we are looking to invest in the next generation of sound. Our Artist Sponsorship Program is designed to remove the barriers that often hold back independent creators.

We are building a new model of support committed to providing emerging artists with the professional resources, studio time, and equipment they need to launch their careers in the Australian music industry.

Important: The sponsorship program is entirely dependent on the volume of sales through our online store. Every purchase you make directly funds future artist support. Get the message out and support our retail operations!

What Sponsorship May Include
  • 01
    Professional Studio Recording Future
    Sponsored access to Oz Soundz Studios for tracking, mixing, and mastering sessions
  • 02
    Quality Gear & Equipment
    High-quality instruments and audio gear from Oz Soundz Music & Audio
  • 03
    Label Support & Distribution Future
    Selected artists may receive assistance from Oz Soundz Records
